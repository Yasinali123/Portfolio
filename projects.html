Yasinova

YASINOVA is a smart construction marketplace and housing services platform designed to simplify the construction process by connecting buyers, suppliers, transporters, builders, engineers, and interior designers in one digital ecosystem. The platform helps users buy and sell construction materials such as sand, bricks, steel, and cement with transparent pricing, verified suppliers, and reliable delivery support. Along with material sourcing, YASINOVA also provides services like 2D house plans, 3D models, walkthroughs, interior design, and basic Vastu-based planning assistance. The main goal of the platform is to solve problems like unorganized supply chains, illegal material sourcing, lack of trust, and delivery inefficiencies while empowering rural suppliers and creating a modern, technology-driven construction ecosystem.
